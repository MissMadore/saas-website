Artist of the Month

Bobbie Gentry exemplifies the spirit of the American singer-songwriter. Upon its release in 1967, her hit single “Ode to Billie Joe” knocked the Beatles’ “All you Need is Love” out of the #1 Billboard spot. She is a true gem, and her sensual, southern-fried voice was all we needed to make her May’s Artist of the Month.

Modern Tributes

Chuck Alder channels the very finest qualities of the ‘60s in his music. With strong, smooth Motown vibes downplayed into mellow synth-scapes, we highly recommend Alder to any and all who love the likes of Marvin Gaye and Chuck Berry. Ideally listened to on a long drive straight out of the city.
